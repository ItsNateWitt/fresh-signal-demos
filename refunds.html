Refund Policy

The short version

Cancel the monthly whenever you want, with no penalty and no notice period. The one-time setup fee is non-refundable once I start building — because that's when the work actually gets done. Either way, you keep your website and your domain.

The $500 setup fee

  • It's due at signing, before I begin building.
  • Before I begin work: ask and I'll refund it in full, no questions.
  • Once I begin building: it's non-refundable. The work is done up front — the site is built, your Google profile is fixed, the assistant is configured — so there's nothing left to give back.
  • If I fail to deliver your site and I'm the reason, I refund you. That's on me, not you.

The $99 monthly

  • It starts 30 days after your site goes live, then bills monthly.
  • Cancel any time — email or text me. No penalty, no notice period, no cancellation fee.
  • Your service runs through the end of the month you've already paid for.
  • I don't refund partial months, and I don't bill you again after you cancel.

What you keep if you cancel

Your website files and your domain name. They're yours — I hand over the files and transfer the domain to you at no charge.

One thing to know: hosting, the customer assistant, and upkeep stop when you cancel. Your site will go offline unless you move it to your own hosting. I'll tell you exactly how, and I'll help you do it.

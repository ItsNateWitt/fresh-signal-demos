The name is the promise.

You're supposed to leave so full you mean it — plate clean, heart happy, ready for a nap.

The So Full Cafe is a family-owned kitchen in North Little Rock cooking the Southern soul food this family grew up on. Everything's made from scratch: the oxtails braise low and slow, the greens simmer all morning, the mac & cheese comes out of the oven bubbling. Sunday, the plates go out till they're gone — and Black Swan Catering carries the rest of the week.

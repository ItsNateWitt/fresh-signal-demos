01The damage

What the storm left behind

After the wind and hail, most roofs don't fail all at once — they leak quietly. Lifted shingles, bruised granules, a nail pop you'd never spot from the driveway. Left alone, it becomes a ceiling stain by spring. We climb up and find it while it's still cheap to fix.

Our inspectors are HAAG certified — the standard adjusters themselves are trained on. We photograph and measure every slope, write it up, and walk your insurance claim with you so nothing legitimate gets denied. You get a straight answer: repair, or replace, and why.

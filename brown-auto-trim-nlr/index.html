The same shop, the same hands

Stitched in-house, the old way.

This is a name-on-the-door trim shop, not a chain counter. The panel comes off, the pattern gets cut, and it goes back on the machine with a double-needle French seam — the seam you feel hold up years down the road.

North Little Rock has sent Brown’s its seats, tops and “most anything” for four decades. Ask for Tom Brown — his name is still the one on the shop.

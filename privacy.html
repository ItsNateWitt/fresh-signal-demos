Privacy Policy

The short version

I collect what I need to build and run your site, I never sell it, and if you leave I give it back and delete my copies.

What I collect

  • From business owners: your name, business name, address, phone, email, and the content you send me (photos, prices, hours) so I can build your site.
  • From your customers, on your site: only what they choose to type — a name, a phone number, a question — so the assistant can answer them or pass them to you.
  • Payments: handled entirely by Stripe. I never see or store your card number.

What I do with it

Run your website and your assistant. That's it.

What I never do

I will never sell, rent, or share your data, or your customers' data, with anyone. I don't use it to advertise to anyone. I don't build a list out of it.

Text messages

Messages go only to people who explicitly agreed to receive them. Reply STOP to any message to opt out, or HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply; frequency varies. I will not message an imported list of your existing customers.

Who else touches the data

Only the services needed to keep your site running — hosting, the AI model provider that powers the assistant, and Stripe for payments. They process it on my instruction and nothing more.

If you cancel

I hand your data and your site files back to you and delete my copies.

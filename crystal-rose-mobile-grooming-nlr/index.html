Why mobile

Grooming that's gentle on purpose.

A busy salon is loud, crowded and full of strange dogs. Crystal Rose is the opposite: a calm, one-on-one spa parked in your own driveway, where the only pet in the room is yours.

No cages. No hours in a kennel waiting for a turn. Just personal, unhurried care — the kind of morning your best friend actually looks forward to.

What we do

Every pup leaves fresh, fluffy & proud.

Full-service grooming from a warm bath to a breed-perfect finish — plus a supervised day of play. Gentle hands, no rushing, and a beauty parlor that treats your dog like the regular they are.

01

Baths & de-shedding

Warm, sudsy baths, a thorough blow-out and de-shed, ears and eyes tidied — the reset that makes the whole coat come alive.

02

Breed-specific cuts & styling

The right cut for the coat — poodle trims, doodle teddy-bear faces, terrier tidy-ups. Shaped clean, finished soft.

03

Nail trims & tidy-ups

Quick, calm nail trims and paw tidies — pop in for a between-groom touch-up any time we're open.

04

Doggy daycare & play

A full day of supervised play, naps and socializing while you work — your dog comes home happy and worn out (in the best way).
